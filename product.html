The product that we are trying to make has to be both easy and safe to use while remaining accesible.

How it works ?

The product consists in a fast spinning blade that cuts plastic down to pieces. It is going to be moroized so all you have to do is to toss a bottle and press a button.
It might sound dangerous for small children to use and we designed it with that in mind. The product is made so that you don't come in contact with the spinning blade.
